It tells your app to pause, reset, or update the view. This makes your app feel professional and reliable. It handles the manual work so you do not have to write extra code.

The tool now monitors your wallet activity in the background. It sends a signal to the website every time your status changes.

## 🔍 How it works

The tool operates on three core principles.

### Account monitoring
When you change your account inside the Phantom wallet, this tool detects the swap instantly. It informs the website. The website then updates the balance and history shown on your screen. You avoid seeing data from the wrong wallet.

### Chain switching
Switching from one network to another, like Ethereum to a test network, confuses many websites. This tool forces a refresh of the connection request. The website sees the new network preference and adjusts its settings. 

### Graceful disconnection
If you lock your wallet or choose to disconnect, the website often keeps trying to send data. This causes errors. This tool triggers a disconnect event so the website stops trying to reach your wallet. This keeps your private keys safe and stops error pop-ups from appearing.

## 🛡️ Privacy and security

This software acts as a bridge between your browser and the website. It does not store your seed phrase. It does not record your private keys. It only reads the status of your connection. It acts as a passive observer. It never takes funds or moves assets. All processing happens inside your browser. No data leaves your machine.

*   Verify permissions: Make sure you allowed the tool to read data on the websites you visit.
